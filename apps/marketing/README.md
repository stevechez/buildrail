# BuildRail HQ

## Purpose

The public-facing home of BuildRail.

BuildRail HQ introduces the BuildRail ecosystem, communicates the platform vision, explains products, captures leads, and guides contractors toward the tools that help them run and grow their businesses.

## Role in the Ecosystem

BuildRail HQ is the front door to the entire platform.

It is responsible for:

- Brand positioning
- Product discovery
- Marketing content
- Pricing
- Customer acquisition
- Sign-up flows
- Documentation entry points

## Products Showcased

- BuildRail Sites
- BuildRail Field Intelligence
- BuildRail Vault
- BuildRail SiteVerdict
- BuildRail LocalProof
- BuildRail Growth System
- BuildRail Estimator

## Technology

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Shadcn UI

## Future Direction

BuildRail HQ will evolve into the primary marketing and onboarding experience for the entire BuildRail platform.
